Teachers Information

A goal without a plan is just a wish. If we are anything like the average working person, we can expect to change careers five to seven times in our life. That’s a lot of change, the best way to be prepared for it is to make a plan! Having a career plan is vital to our career success. The most successful people, such as Tim ferries, Jeff bezos and Mark Zuckerberg , this people regularly come up with plans for their success and look where its gotten them! I think that 3 steps we can be follow to reach our goal and success to our life. 1. Self-reflection: It’s easy to neglect to reflecting on our career when we are so busy chugging along, but taking the time to think about our current situation and the path we want to be on is crucial to a productive career plan. Research even shows reflection is key to success and it increases productivity and performance. 2. Goal setting: Self reflection will lead us to identifying what kind of career we want. Now its time to figure out how to get there. Setting goals is the key to a successful career plan. But what kind of goals we are creat? We already probably know about Smart goals. These are specific, measurable, attainable, relevant and timebound goals that all set us up for success in our goal setting and achievement. Infact studies show smart goals really do work. But in order to successful, we need to write them down and share them with a friend or a coworker. Seventy-six
